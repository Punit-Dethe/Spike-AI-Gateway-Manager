Usage Example

from openai import OpenAI

client = OpenAI(
    base_url="http://localhost:6969/v1",
    api_key="dummy"
)

response = client.chat.completions.create(
    model="gemini-2.0-flash",
    messages=[{"role": "user", "content": "Hello!"}]
)

print(response.choices[0].message.content)

Setup Guide

How to get started with Spike

Getting Your Tokens

  1. Open Gemini

    Navigate to https://gemini.google.com (MUST be HTTPS, not HTTP)

  2. Sign In

    Log in with your Google account that has Gemini access

  3. Open DevTools

    Press F12 or right-click and select "Inspect"

  4. Navigate to Cookies

    Go to: Application → Cookies → https://gemini.google.com

    ⚠ IMPORTANT: Make sure you're looking at cookies for https://gemini.google.com (with HTTPS), NOT google.com or any other domain!

  5. Copy Tokens

    Find and copy the ENTIRE VALUE of these two cookies:

    • __Secure-1PSID (starts with "g." and is very long)
    • __Secure-1PSIDTS (also very long)

    ⚠ CRITICAL: Copy the COMPLETE value - these tokens are typically 200-500+ characters long. If your token is short, you didn't copy it all!

  6. Configure Spike

    Go to "Token Settings" tab, paste your tokens, and click "Save Tokens"

  7. Initialize

    Click "Initialize Client" and wait for confirmation (may take 10-30 seconds)

  8. Verify Authentication

    Test in the "Chat Test" tab. If it works, your chat history should appear in gemini.google.com/app

    ⚠ If history doesn't show: Your tokens are wrong or expired. Get fresh tokens!

Troubleshooting

🚨 Not Authenticated / Free Version Only

Symptoms: Shows "Gemini 3 Flash free version", chat history doesn't appear in gemini.google.com/app

Solution: Your cookies are WRONG or EXPIRED. Get fresh cookies from https://gemini.google.com (with HTTPS). Make sure you copy the ENTIRE cookie value (200-500+ characters). Cookies expire after ~30 days.

Authentication Failed

Get fresh tokens from gemini.google.com. Make sure you're copying from the correct domain (https://gemini.google.com, not google.com). Tokens expire after ~30 days.

Client Not Initializing

Verify tokens are correct and you're signed in to Gemini in your browser. Check that you copied the COMPLETE token value (they're very long!).

400 Bad Request

Make sure to initialize the client before sending requests.

Cookies Keep Expiring

Spike Lite has auto-refresh enabled! Once initialized, cookies will automatically refresh in the background. You shouldn't need to update them manually unless you restart the app.
