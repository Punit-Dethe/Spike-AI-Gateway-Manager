Usage Example

from openai import OpenAI

client = OpenAI(
    base_url="http://localhost:6969/v1",
    api_key="dummy"
)

response = client.chat.completions.create(
    model="gemini-2.0-flash",
    messages=[{"role": "user", "content": "Hello!"}]
)

print(response.choices[0].message.content)

Setup Guide

How to get started with Spike

Getting Your Tokens

  1. Open Gemini

    Navigate to gemini.google.com in Chrome or Edge

  2. Sign In

    Log in with your Google account

  3. Open DevTools

    Press F12 or right-click and select "Inspect"

  4. Navigate to Cookies

    Go to: Application → Cookies → https://gemini.google.com

  5. Copy Tokens

    Find and copy these two cookies:

    • __Secure-1PSID
    • __Secure-1PSIDTS
  6. Configure Spike

    Go to "Token Settings" tab, paste your tokens, and click "Save Tokens"

  7. Initialize

    Click "Initialize Client" and wait for confirmation

Troubleshooting

Authentication Failed

Get fresh tokens from gemini.google.com. Tokens expire after ~30 days.

Client Not Initializing

Verify tokens are correct and you're signed in to Gemini in your browser.

400 Bad Request

Make sure to initialize the client before sending requests.
